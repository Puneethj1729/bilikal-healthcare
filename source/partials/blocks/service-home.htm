Our services

From sore throats and earaches to sinusitis or hearing loss, the Ear, Nose, and Throat Center is equipped to handle all your otolaryngology needs. To help you understand your options, we’ve included descriptions of some of our leading services.

HEARING

The ear houses both hearing and balance senses.We offer comprehensive evaluations to help manage and treat your ear disorder from hearing aids to ear surgery..

NASAL/SINUS

Nasal and sinus problems can prevent you from being active and enjoying life. Our physicians are experts in the medical and surgical management of conditions of the nose and sinus.

PEDIATRIC

Ear, nose, and throat issues may present differently in children than in adults. Our physicians provide the appropriate pediatric management and comfortable treatments for your child’s needs.

THYROID

The thyroid gland is vulnerable to a host of medical problems, many causing noticeable swelling in the neck. Our expert physicians treat acute and advanced thyroid conditions.

SLEEP APNEA

Sleep apnea may lead to daytime fatigue, relationship problems, and other medical issues. If your partner tells you that you snore, make an appointment today.

SAME DAY VISITS

If you’re having problems with your ear, nose, or throat today, we’re here to help with immediate assistance by offering same day appointments.We can help you instantly.

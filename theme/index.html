Our services

From sore throats and earaches to sinusitis or hearing loss, the Ear, Nose, and Throat Center is equipped to handle all your otolaryngology needs. To help you understand your options, we’ve included descriptions of some of our leading services.

HEARING

The ear houses both hearing and balance senses.We offer comprehensive evaluations to help manage and treat your ear disorder from hearing aids to ear surgery..

NASAL/SINUS

Nasal and sinus problems can prevent you from being active and enjoying life. Our physicians are experts in the medical and surgical management of conditions of the nose and sinus.

PEDIATRIC

Ear, nose, and throat issues may present differently in children than in adults. Our physicians provide the appropriate pediatric management and comfortable treatments for your child’s needs.

THYROID

The thyroid gland is vulnerable to a host of medical problems, many causing noticeable swelling in the neck. Our expert physicians treat acute and advanced thyroid conditions.

SLEEP APNEA

Sleep apnea may lead to daytime fatigue, relationship problems, and other medical issues. If your partner tells you that you snore, make an appointment today.

We served over 5000+ Patients

Bilikal ENT clinic is a comprehensive center equipped to deal with all ENT problems. Here, the Synergy between technical innovation and well trained manpower aims to provide the best possible treatment.It is well equipped with the latest diagnostic technology. Our scope of management varies from common ENT problems to more complex scenarios such as breathing difficulties, drooling in children, snoring and sleep apnoea, and unusual neck swellings. We also work with neurologists and department of rehabilitation for many neurological and geriatric conditions, treating spasmodic dysphonia, vocal cord tremor & other laryngeal movement disorder patients with video assisted injections. The Department also deals with various airway conditions including laryngotracheal stenosis & all ear diseases and tympanomastoid surgeries. We also provides audiology, voice and speech therapy as allied services for voice problems, stammering, autism and other speech problems. We also have a unit dealing with swallowing problems. The Clinic is well-equipped to take care of all Ear, Nose and Throat emergencies.
